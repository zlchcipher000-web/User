## Logo

The EduFund logo consists of:
- **Icon**: A stacked book/graduation cap icon in white, positioned left
- **Wordmark**: "EduFund" in bold white text
- **Tagline**: "PARENT / STUDENT PORTAL" in small uppercase tracking below the wordmark

The logo is placed in the sidebar header area with white text on the navy background.
